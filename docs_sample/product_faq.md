# Product FAQ

## Account Opening

**Q: How do I open a NovaBanque current account?**
A: You can open a NovaBanque account entirely online. Download the NovaBanque app,
tap "Open Account", and follow the identity verification steps. You will need a valid
government-issued photo ID, proof of address (utility bill or bank statement dated within
90 days), and a video selfie for biometric verification. Approval typically takes 24–48
business hours.

**Q: What documents do I need to open a business account?**
A: Business accounts require the company's certificate of incorporation, a list of
beneficial owners holding more than 25% of shares, and personal identity documents for
each beneficial owner. A compliance officer will review your application within 5 business
days.

**Q: Can I open an account if I am not an EU resident?**
A: Currently, NovaBanque accounts are available to residents of EU/EEA countries only.
Support for additional jurisdictions is planned for 2026.

## Cards and Payments

**Q: What types of cards does NovaBanque offer?**
A: NovaBanque offers three card types:
- **Standard Debit Card** — linked to your current account, no annual fee.
- **Premium Debit Card** — includes travel insurance, airport lounge access, and priority
  support. Annual fee of €99.
- **Virtual Card** — instant issuance for online purchases; no physical card issued.

**Q: My card was declined at a foreign ATM. What should I do?**
A: First, check that international ATM withdrawals are enabled in the app under
Settings → Card Controls → International ATM. If enabled and the problem persists,
contact our 24/7 support line at +48 800 123 456 or use in-app chat.

**Q: How do I dispute a transaction?**
A: Navigate to Transactions in the app, select the disputed transaction, and tap
"Dispute this transaction". Fill in the reason and submit. We investigate disputes
within 5–10 business days. For urgent cases, call our support line.

## Fees

**Q: What fees does NovaBanque charge?**
A: Standard account maintenance is free. Fees that may apply:
- Outgoing SWIFT transfer: €15 flat fee + correspondent bank charges.
- Foreign currency conversion: 0.5% markup on interbank rate.
- Paper statement request: €3 per statement.
- Replacement physical card: €10.

**Q: Are there fees for SEPA transfers?**
A: Incoming and outgoing SEPA transfers are free of charge for all account types.
Same-day SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst) is also free up to €100,000.

## Security

**Q: How does NovaBanque protect my money?**
A: Customer deposits are protected up to €100,000 per depositor under the Polish
Deposit Guarantee Scheme (BFG), consistent with EU Directive 2014/49/EU. All funds are
held in segregated accounts at Tier-1 partner banks.

**Q: What should I do if I suspect fraud on my account?**
A: Immediately freeze your card via the app (Settings → Card Controls → Freeze Card)
and contact our 24/7 fraud hotline. Do not share your PIN, one-time passwords, or
security codes with anyone — NovaBanque will never ask for these.
