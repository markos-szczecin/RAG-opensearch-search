# Account Upgrade Procedures

## Overview

This procedure outlines the standardized process for upgrading NovaBanque customer accounts from basic tiers to premium account levels. All operations team members must follow these guidelines to ensure consistent service delivery and compliance with our account management framework.

## Account Tier Definitions

NovaBanque offers three primary account tiers:

- **Basic Account**: Standard features with monthly transaction limits and standard interest rates
- **Premium Account**: Enhanced features including higher transaction limits, priority customer support, and competitive interest rates
- **Elite Account**: Full-featured access with unlimited transactions, dedicated account managers, and premium benefits

## Eligibility Requirements

Before initiating an upgrade, verify that the customer meets the following criteria:

1. **Account Age**: Customer must maintain an active account for a minimum of 30 days
2. **Account Standing**: No unresolved disputes, fraudulent activity flags, or compliance concerns in the past 12 months
3. **Minimum Balance**: Customer must maintain the required minimum balance threshold for the requested tier
4. **Verification Status**: Customer must have completed full identity verification and KYC documentation
5. **No Pending Actions**: No outstanding security reviews or investigation holds on the account

## Upgrade Request Process

### Step 1: Request Reception
- Customers may request upgrades through the mobile app, web portal, or by contacting customer service
- Document the upgrade request in the CRM system with timestamp and customer ID
- Verify the customer's identity using multi-factor authentication

### Step 2: Eligibility Verification
- Run automated eligibility checks against the account database
- Review transaction history for the past 90 days
- Confirm compliance status and absence of risk flags
- Document any exceptions requiring manual review

### Step 3: Customer Communication
- Send confirmation email detailing upgrade benefits, new fees, and terms
- Provide a 24-hour review period for the customer to confirm or cancel
- Include links to updated account agreements and fee schedules

### Step 4: Account Configuration
- Once confirmed, update account tier classification in the core banking system
- Enable enhanced features and adjust transaction limits immediately
- Apply applicable promotional rates or incentives if applicable
- Generate updated account documentation

### Step 5: Notification and Documentation
- Send welcome notification confirming upgrade completion
- Provide updated account statements reflecting new tier status
- File all related documentation in the customer's account folder
- Record completion in the upgrade tracking log

## Special Circumstances

**Conditional Upgrades**: Customers with minor compliance notes may receive provisional upgrades pending resolution within 30 days.

**Expedited Upgrades**: High-value customers or those referred through partnership programs may qualify for accelerated processing with manager approval.

**Trial Periods**: Certain promotional campaigns authorize 14-day trial periods for Premium features before charging applicable fees.

## Quality Assurance

All upgrades undergo random audit sampling on a weekly basis. Audit teams verify:

- Proper eligibility documentation
- Accurate system configuration
- Timely customer notification
- Correct fee application

## Escalation and Support

If technical issues arise during upgrade, immediately escalate to the Technical Operations team with account number and error details. For customer disputes regarding eligibility, refer to the Compliance department.
