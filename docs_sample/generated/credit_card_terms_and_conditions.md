# Credit Card Terms and Conditions – Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**What are the main features of the NovaBanque Credit Card?**

The NovaBanque Credit Card offers a flexible spending limit, **reward points** on every purchase, and interest-free periods on balance transfers. Cardholders enjoy 24/7 digital management through our mobile app and web portal, plus fraud protection and purchase security guarantees.

**What are the eligibility requirements to apply?**

You must be at least 18 years old, a resident of an eligible country, and meet our credit assessment criteria. We require proof of income or employment, valid identification, and a verified bank account for automatic payments.

## Fees and Interest Rates

**What fees should I expect?**

Our standard annual fee is **€49**, waived in your first year. We charge no foreign transaction fees within the EU. Late payment fees are €25 per occurrence, and cash advance fees are 3% of the amount withdrawn (minimum €3). There are no fees for balance transfers during promotional periods.

**How is interest calculated on purchases?**

The standard purchase APR is **18.99%**. However, new cardholders receive a 0% introductory rate for the first 6 months on purchases. After this period, interest accrues daily on any remaining balance. Your annual percentage rate (APR) may vary based on creditworthiness.

**What is the grace period?**

You have a **25-day grace period** from your statement closing date. If you pay your full balance within this window, no interest charges apply to purchases. Interest begins accruing immediately on cash advances, regardless of the grace period.

## Rewards and Benefits

**How do the reward points work?**

You earn **1 point per €1 spent** on standard purchases and **2 points per €1** on dining, travel, and entertainment transactions. Points never expire and can be redeemed for cash back, travel bookings, or merchandise through our rewards portal. One point equals approximately €0.01 in redemption value.

**Are there any annual benefits or bonuses?**

Yes! You receive a **birthday bonus** of 500 points each year, plus quarterly bonus point multipliers during promotional periods. Cardholders also enjoy complimentary travel insurance, emergency cash advances abroad, and priority customer support.

## Payments and Account Management

**How do I make payments?**

Payments can be made via our mobile app, online banking portal, automatic bank transfer, or telephone. We accept payments from any bank account worldwide. **Automatic payments** are free and help ensure you never miss a due date.

**What happens if I miss a payment?**

A late payment fee applies (€25), and your interest rate may increase. After **30 days**, the delinquency is reported to credit bureaus, potentially affecting your credit score. We encourage cardholders to enable automatic minimum payments to avoid this situation.

**Can I increase my credit limit?**

Yes. After demonstrating responsible usage for at least 6 months, you can request a limit increase through your online account or by contacting our customer service team. Increases are subject to credit review.

## Security and Fraud Protection

**Is my card protected against fraud?**

Absolutely. All NovaBanque credit cards include **zero-liability fraud protection**. If unauthorized charges occur, report them within 60 days and we'll investigate at no cost to you. Your card will be replaced immediately upon verification of fraud.

For additional assistance, contact our customer service team at **1-800-NOVA-HELP** or visit your account dashboard.
